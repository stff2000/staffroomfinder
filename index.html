About the website

Staffroom Finder is a tool designed specifically for NMAMIT students to help them locate staffrooms, labs, and other important facilities on campus. With this tool, students can easily find the location of their classes, labs, and staffrooms, as well as access important notes and resources. The Staffroom Finder is a convenient and user-friendly resource that helps students navigate the campus and stay organized.
